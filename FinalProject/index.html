Hillsboro Beach

Hillsboro Beach is a quiet town with the landmark lighthouse overlooking the inlet. On this day, a storm was brewing out in the ocean on my walk. I could not stay long since the storm rolled within 30 minutes. The ocean and the sky both looked menacing, and all you can hear on the deserted beach was the waves crashing. If you want to avoid South Florida’s tourist rush, this is the beach to come to enjoy solidarity in peace. I like to come to reset and find inspiration.
